Architecture, Interior, & Construction

At Ace Integrated Studio, we offer a comprehensive range of architecture, interior design, and construction services for both corporate and residential projects. With expertise across multiple sectors, including shed construction and office furnishing for corporate leasing, we ensure that each project is designed with creativity and built with precision.

Operating in key locations like Noida Sector 63, Noida Sector 136, Wavecity ghaziabad, and Jaypee Greens Noida, Delhi NCR we provide tailored solutions to meet the specific needs of these dynamic areas. From the initial concept to the final completion, our focus is on delivering innovative designs that seamlessly integrate functionality, aesthetics, and structural integrity.

Architectural Design

Our architectural design services balance artistic vision with engineering precision, crafting spaces that reflect both purpose and elegance. Whether you're looking for corporate office designs or residential architecture, our sustainable design approach ensures every project remains environmentally conscious and energy-efficient.

Corporate Interiors

  • Optimized Layouts: Maximize functionality and promote smooth workflow.
  • Brand Integration: Reinforce corporate culture with branded, tailored designs.
  • Ergonomics and Comfort: Prioritize employee comfort, health, and productivity.
  • Flexible Spaces: Create adaptable areas for collaboration, meetings, and privacy.
  • High-End Office Furnishing: Provide stylish receptions and fully-furnished leasing setups, merging aesthetics with utility.

Residential Interiors

  • Custom Designs: Personalize each space to reflect your unique style and comfort.
  • Luxury Living: Create high-end interiors for premium homes like Jaypee Greens and Wavecity Noida.
  • Smart Interiors: Integrate smart technology and lighting for enhanced functionality and ambiance.
  • Space Optimization: Design rooms for maximum efficiency and comfort, from living areas to kitchens.
  • Timeless Aesthetics: Blend contemporary trends with timeless elegance for lasting appeal.

Construction Services

Our shed construction and full-scale residential and corporate developments are executed with the highest standards of quality. We manage all construction phases, ensuring timely delivery and adherence to safety protocols. Our expertise spans from large corporate buildings to bespoke residential homes, providing free site visits and online consultancy to assist clients through every step of the process.
