Drafting & Design Services

At Ace Integrated Studio, our Drafting & Design Services are rooted in delivering excellence across every stage of the project lifecycle. We offer a comprehensive range of services that cater to both design precision and functionality.

Corporate Interiors

  • Scan to BIM | Point Cloud to BIM: Our Scan to BIM services focus on converting point clouds uploaded by clients into highly accurate and detailed BIM models. This process captures existing conditions with precision, making it ideal for renovation projects where understanding the current structure is crucial. By transforming point cloud data into actionable models, we facilitate seamless integration of new designs with existing conditions, ultimately leading to improved project outcomes.
  • BIM Modeling | Architectural | Structural | MEPF: We provide comprehensive BIM Modeling services that encompass Architectural, Structural, and MEPF (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, and Fire Protection) systems. Our modeling services reach up to LOD 500, ensuring that every detail is accurately represented. This level of detail supports complex and large-scale projects, enabling effective communication among stakeholders and minimizing risks associated with misinterpretations during the construction phase.
  • Revit Library Creation: Our Revit Library Creation service involves the development of custom Revit families and libraries tailored to meet the unique specifications of our clients. These libraries are designed for easy integration into BIM models, streamlining the design process and enhancing overall project efficiency. By providing clients with standardized and specialized components, we facilitate quicker design iterations and improved collaboration across teams.
  • BIM Coordination and Clash Detection: We specialize in BIM Coordination and Clash Detection, ensuring that architectural, structural, and MEPF systems are well-coordinated. Our process involves thorough analysis and detection of potential conflicts early in the design phase. By addressing these issues upfront, we help avoid costly delays and rework during construction, leading to smoother project execution and enhanced collaboration among disciplines.
  • 4D, 5D & 6D BIM: Our 4D, 5D, and 6D BIM services incorporate advanced project management methodologies. 4D BIM enables visualization of construction timelines, enhancing project planning and workflow optimization. 5D BIM links real-time cost estimation to the BIM model, facilitating effective budget management. 6D BIM integrates facility management data, providing clients with a comprehensive overview of the building’s lifecycle, including maintenance and operational needs.
  • Revit Construction Drawings: We provide precise Revit Construction Drawings essential for effective communication and execution in construction projects. Our drawings ensure clarity and completeness, which are critical in aligning various stakeholders. They are crafted to comply with industry standards and cater to diverse project types, aiding in the seamless transition from design to construction.
  • Architectural Interior Modeling: Our Architectural Interior Modeling service translates design concepts into detailed and visually engaging models. This service encompasses everything from conceptual design to the development of intricate interior plans, reflecting both the aesthetic and functional goals of a space. By utilizing advanced modeling techniques, we bring your vision to life, ensuring that every detail aligns with your expectations.
  • CoBie BIM Services: Ace Integrated Studio offers specialized CoBie BIM Services focused on enhancing facility management capabilities. Our consultancy helps clients efficiently manage their assets throughout the building’s lifecycle by integrating BIM into facility operations. This service not only streamlines maintenance processes but also provides valuable insights for long-term asset management.
  • Quantity Extraction & Shop Drawings: Our Quantity Extraction and Shop Drawings service delivers detailed schedules and drawings, essential for accurate project execution. We provide precise quantity take-offs that aid in budgeting and resource planning, while our shop drawings facilitate the construction process, ensuring that all elements are accounted for and aligned with project specifications.
  • CAD Drafting Services: We offer comprehensive CAD Drafting Services to support the digitization of projects. Our skilled team produces high-quality CAD drawings that ensure precision and clarity in design documentation. This service is essential for creating reliable documentation that aligns with industry standards and meets project requirements.
  • CAD Drafting to BIM Conversion: Our CAD Drafting to BIM Conversion service focuses on transforming 2D CAD drawings into accurate BIM models. This conversion process enhances the detail and accuracy of project documentation, allowing for better integration with existing BIM workflows. By transitioning to BIM, clients benefit from improved collaboration and project efficiency.
  • Site Modeling: Our Site Modeling services specialize in creating detailed models for site and landscaping design. This includes analyzing site conditions and producing visual representations that support effective planning and design decisions. Our expertise in site modeling ensures that outdoor spaces are thoughtfully integrated with the overall project vision.

BIM SERVICES | STANDARDS FOLLOWED

At ACE Integrated Studio, we adhere to established BIM standards and codes, including IS 19650 and BS PAS 1192. Our commitment to following these standards ensures that our BIM services meet the highest quality and compliance benchmarks, fostering trust and confidence in our project deliverables.

AR-VR & Visualization Services

Our AR-VR & Visualization Services at Ace Integrated Studio create immersive experiences that transform how clients interact with designs.

  • Augmented Reality (AR) for Architecture & Interiors: We bring designs to life by overlaying digital models onto real-world spaces, allowing clients to visualize how architectural elements and interior designs will fit within their environment.
  • Virtual Reality (VR) for Architecture & Interiors: Our VR services immerse clients in virtual environments where they can explore and experience designs in a fully interactive way, offering a deeper understanding of spatial layouts and materials.
  • Extended Reality (XR) Meetings: We offer real-time collaboration through XR meetings, enabling clients to interact with virtual models, suggest changes, and see updates in real-time, providing a seamless design and review experience.
  • High-Quality 3D Renders: Our team produces photorealistic 3D renders using the latest visualization technologies, providing clear and detailed visual representations of projects.
  • Walkthroughs & Flythroughs: We offer dynamic 3D walkthroughs and flythroughs that allow clients to take virtual tours of their projects, showcasing every aspect of the design with clarity and precision.

AI in Design

At Ace Integrated Studio, we embrace AI in Design to enhance creativity and efficiency in the design process. Our AI-powered services integrate cutting-edge technology with human intelligence to achieve superior results.

  • AI-Based Real-Time Efficient Designing with Virtual Consultancy: We utilize AI to streamline the design process, offering real-time design iterations and solutions through virtual consultancy, reducing timeframes while maintaining accuracy and creativity.
  • Integration of Artificial and Human Intelligence: By merging artificial intelligence with the expertise of our design professionals, we create innovative, efficient, and sustainable design solutions that push the boundaries of traditional architecture and interior design.
